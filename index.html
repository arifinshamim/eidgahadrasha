Principal's Message

Dear Parents/Guardians,

On behalf of all in Eidgah Adarsha Shiska Niketon (EASN), may I extend a warm welcome to you. This is the new school diary for the academic year 2017-18. As you all know that this is a very important piece of document that students must have in their possession. It contains valuable information regarding the school and its practices. There are general rules along with specific ones for various matters such as grades, promotion, chart for routine and records, information about late attendance, homework, sample signatures of student and parents. Changing schools is a big step for any youngster, and the purpose of this handbook is to help you and your daughter/son take the step with more ease and confidence. The handbook contains information about how the school operates, about the courses and other opportunities available. All students are required to bring this diary to school every day for academic and identification purposes. Blank space for signatures and addresses must be duly filled up by respective students and their parents.

We would like to encourage you to make and maintain a good personal contact with the school. A strong home-school link is a very important part of a youngster’s education. In this school, we are going to introduce Class-tune through which parents/students can digitally receive announcements, feedbacks, test/quiz dates, information about class attendance and absence, etc. As a parent, you will be able to view your child/ward’s activity at school. Students’ progress and activity will be monitored by faculty to ensure a safe, secure and controlled environment. If you have questions or feedback regarding class-tune please email us at (sjisbd2017@gmai.com).

Yours sincerely,

Bro. Robi Purification, CSC

Principal
