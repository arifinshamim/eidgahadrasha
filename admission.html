Admission

St. Joseph International School (SJIS) enrolls both boys and girls in Cambridge Curriculum (academic year July-June). Application Forms for admission into class III are available in the school office in the middle of March every year.

A child 8-9 years of age, who has completed grade II is eligible for admission.

Admission is offered if a candidate qualifies through interview and written test held in the first week in of April.

For selected applications ‘Admission Forms’ are available at Office.
